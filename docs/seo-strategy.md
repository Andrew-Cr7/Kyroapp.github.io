# Kyro SEO Strategy

# Purpose

This document defines Kyro's SEO strategy.

The objective is to build a sustainable source of organic traffic from people actively searching for gym access while travelling.

SEO should support:

* Waitlist growth
* User acquisition
* Brand awareness
* Future bookings
* Long-term revenue growth

The goal is not simply traffic.

The goal is qualified visitors who are likely to become Kyro users.

---

# SEO Mission

Become the most trusted resource on the internet for finding gym access while travelling.

When someone searches for:

* Gym day pass
* Tourist gym access
* Gym while travelling
* Best gyms in a city
* Gym access for digital nomads

Kyro should be present.

---

# SEO Principles

## 1. Solve Real Problems

Every page should answer a real question.

Avoid creating pages purely for keywords.

Content should be genuinely useful.

---

## 2. Focus on Search Intent

Understand what the visitor actually wants.

Examples:

Search:

"gym day pass london"

Intent:

Find somewhere to train in London.

Not:

Read a history lesson about gyms.

---

## 3. Create Better Content Than Competitors

Do not create content simply because competitors have it.

Create content that is:

* More useful
* More relevant
* More detailed
* Better structured

---

## 4. Build Topical Authority

The goal is to become the authority on:

Fitness while travelling.

Not:

General fitness.

Not:

General travel.

The overlap between both.

---

Purpose:

Capture users searching for gym access in specific locations.

Examples:

/tokyo-gym-day-passes

/seoul-gym-day-passes

/singapore-gym-day-passes

/barcelona-gym-day-passes

Priority:

Highest

---

## Category 2

Country Pages

Purpose:

Examples:

/gym-day-passes-japan

/gym-day-passes-thailand

/gym-day-passes-south-korea

/gym-day-passes-spain

Priority:

High

---

## Category 3

Travel Fitness Guides

Purpose:

Capture informational traffic.

Examples:

How to Stay Fit While Travelling

Best Gym Chains in Japan

Priority:

High

## Category 4

Comparison Content

Purpose:

Medium

Medium

# Target Audience Keywords

Focus on searches made by:

* Travellers
* Digital nomads
* Business travellers
* Fitness-focused tourists

Avoid trying to rank broadly for:

* Weight loss
* Muscle building
* Workout plans

These are not Kyro's market.

# Backlink Strategy

Goal:

Acquire relevant backlinks.

Priority sources:

* Travel blogs
* Digital nomad blogs
* Fitness blogs
* Gym websites
* Travel newsletters
* Fitness newsletters

Avoid:

Low-quality link schemes.

Spam directories.

Paid backlink networks.

---

# Partner SEO Strategy

Examples:

Partner pages

Partner announcements

Blog mentions

Directory pages

This creates natural authority growth.

---

# Content Production Strategy

Initial goal:

Create foundational content.

Priority order:

1. Major city pages
2. Country pages
3. Travel fitness guides
4. Comparison content
5. Problem-based content

# What We Will Not Do

We will not create content around:

* Workout plans
* Fat loss
* Bodybuilding advice
* Nutrition coaching
* Supplement reviews

Unless directly connected to travelling and gym access.

The goal is topical authority around fitness while travelling.

Not fitness in general.

---

When someone anywhere in the world needs a gym while travelling, Kyro should be one of the first brands they discover.

The website should become the largest and most trusted library of travel fitness and gym access information online.
