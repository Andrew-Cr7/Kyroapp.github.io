# Kyro Business Overview

# Executive Summary

Kyro is a marketplace that helps people access gyms while travelling.

Users can discover and purchase flexible gym access without needing to commit to long-term memberships, navigate complicated sign-up processes or spend hours researching available options.

Kyro connects travellers, digital nomads, business travellers and flexible local users with gyms that offer short-term access.

The long-term vision is to become the global platform for flexible gym access.

Train Anywhere. Train Kyro.

---

# The Problem

Millions of people travel every year while trying to maintain their fitness routine.

However, accessing gyms in unfamiliar locations is often difficult.

Common problems include:

* Long-term membership requirements
* Complicated registration processes
* Local phone number requirements
* Language barriers
* Limited information online
* Unclear visitor policies
* Time spent researching options
* Inconsistent pricing

As a result, many people skip training entirely while travelling.

The issue is not a lack of gyms.

The issue is friction.

---

# Why We Started Kyro

The idea for Kyro came from personal experience.

As frequent travellers and fitness enthusiasts, we repeatedly encountered difficulties finding gyms while travelling internationally.

Examples include:

* Being unable to register without a local phone number
* Spending significant time researching visitor-friendly gyms
* Discovering hidden fees or unclear access policies
* Struggling to compare options across different cities

These experiences highlighted a consistent problem that affects travellers around the world.

Kyro was created to solve that problem.

---

# Mission

To make fitness accessible anywhere in the world by removing the barriers that prevent people from training while travelling.

---

# Vision

A user should be able to arrive anywhere in the world, open Kyro, find a gym, purchase access and start training within minutes.

Our goal is to become the default platform for flexible gym access globally.

---

---

# Business Model

## Current Model

Kyro earns revenue from gym pass sales.

Users purchase access through the platform.

Revenue is shared between:

* Gym partner
* Kyro

Current target commercial structure:

* Approximately 80% to the gym
* Approximately 20% to Kyro

This may evolve over time.

---

## Future Opportunities

Potential future revenue streams include:

* Weekly passes
* Monthly passes
* Featured gym placements
* Corporate partnerships
* Subscription products

These are not current priorities.

---

# Product Overview

Kyro allows users to:

* Search gyms
* View gym information
* Compare options
* Purchase access
* Check into gyms

The platform is focused on reducing friction and simplifying gym discovery.

Kyro is not:

* A fitness coaching platform
* A workout tracking application
* A nutrition platform

The focus is gym access.

---

# Strategic Principles

When making decisions, prioritise:

1. User convenience
2. Simplicity
3. Trust
4. Revenue generation
5. Scalability

Avoid building features that do not directly improve user experience, partner value or business growth.
