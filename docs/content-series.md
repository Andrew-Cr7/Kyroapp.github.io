# Kyro Content Series

## Purpose

This document contains repeatable content series used across:

* Instagram
* TikTok
* YouTube Shorts
* Threads
* X

These series should be reused continuously rather than constantly inventing new content formats.

The goal is to build familiarity, improve audience retention and make content production easier.

---

# Content Mix

Target Split:

* Travel: 40%
* Fitness: 40%
* Founder / Startup: 20%

---

# TRAVEL SERIES

## 1. Nobody Tells You This About...

Purpose:
High reach and curiosity.

Format:

Hook:
"Nobody tells you this about [Country]..."

Observation:
Unexpected insight.

Payoff:
Useful takeaway.

Examples:

* Nobody tells you this about South Korea
* Nobody tells you this about Japan
* Nobody tells you this about Albania
* Nobody tells you this about Peru

Why it works:

* Strong curiosity
* Broad audience appeal
* Easy to repeat

---

# Content Rules

Every piece of content should aim to do at least one of:

* Entertain
* Educate
* Inspire
* Relate

If it does none of these, do not publish it.

---

# Content Goal

The goal is not simply views.

The goal is:

1. Build trust
2. Grow awareness
3. Increase waitlist signups
4. Increase future app downloads
5. Establish Kyro as the brand for fitness while travelling

Train Anywhere. Train Kyro.
