# Kyro Content Strategy

## Purpose

The purpose of content is not simply to gain views.

The primary goal is to:

1. Increase waitlist signups
2. Build trust with travellers and gym-goers
3. Build awareness of the Kyro brand
4. Build the founders' personal brands
5. Acquire users before launch
6. Create a community around fitness and travel

Content should always support at least one of these goals.

---

# Brand Positioning

Kyro sits at the intersection of:

* Travel
* Fitness
* Entrepreneurship

We are not fitness coaches.

We are not travel influencers.

We are building the platform that helps people stay consistent with their fitness while travelling.

Our content should reflect this.

---

# Core Content Pillars

## 1. Travel (40%)

Purpose:
Reach the widest audience possible.

Topics:

* Country observations
* Travel mistakes
* Travel tips
* Budget travel
* Digital nomad lifestyle
* Things nobody tells you about a country
* Cultural differences
* Hidden gems
* Travel stories

Examples:

* Nobody tells you this about South Korea
* The biggest mistake people make in Japan
* £500 travel challenge
* What surprised me most about Peru

Goal:
Awareness and reach.

---

## 2. Fitness (40%)

Purpose:
Attract people who care about staying fit while travelling.

Topics:

* Gym culture around the world
* Training while travelling
* Finding gyms abroad
* Fitness routines on the road
* Gym reviews
* Workout consistency
* Travel fitness tips

Examples:

* The weirdest thing about Japanese gyms
* How I stay in shape while travelling
* The biggest problem with gyms abroad
* Why your fitness disappears when you travel

Goal:
Attract future users.

---

## 3. Founders & Startup (20%)

Purpose:
Build trust and create emotional connection.

Topics:

* Building Kyro
* Startup lessons
* Founder updates
* Wins and failures
* Gym acquisition
* Product development
* Behind the scenes

Examples:

* We got rejected by another gym
* What building a startup actually looks like
* The mistake that cost us 3 months
* Why we started Kyro

Goal:
Build loyal followers and future advocates.

# Content Formats

* TikTok

Highest

High

---

# Content Principles

Every piece of content should achieve at least one of:

* Entertain
* Educate
* Inspire
* Relate

If it does none of these, do not publish it.

---

# Content Style

Tone:

* Authentic
* Founder-led
* Practical
* Honest
* Positive

Avoid:

* Corporate language
* Overly polished content
* Hard selling
* Generic motivational quotes

People should feel like they are following real travellers building a real company.

---

# Calls To Action

Primary CTA:

Join the Kyro waitlist.

Secondary CTA:

Follow the journey.

Examples:

* Join the waitlist
* Follow for travel fitness tips
* Follow our startup journey
* Train Anywhere. Train Kyro.

---

# Success Metrics

Primary Metrics:

* Waitlist signups
* Website visits
* Profile visits
* Followers gained

Secondary Metrics:

* Shares
* Saves
* Comments
* Watch time

Views are useful but should not be treated as the primary success metric.

---

# Current Priority

Current focus is pre-launch growth.

Content should prioritise:

1. Growing awareness
2. Growing the waitlist
3. Building trust
4. Showing the problem Kyro solves

Content should not focus heavily on app features until the product is closer to launch.
