Our Story

We’re two brothers who love fitness and travel. Both having visited 50+ countries and counting, we’ve spent years juggling our workouts with life on the road—searching for gyms in new places, dealing with confusing prices, and skipping workouts when we couldn’t find a place to train.

Having both played sport at a high level—including semi-professional rugby—and being passionate about fitness since our teens, we knew there had to be a better way. So we created Kyro.

And we didn’t build it alone. We teamed up with our old school friend—now our CTO—who’s based in Japan. He’s helping bring Kyro to life, building the tech that makes it possible to find and book gyms wherever you are.

Right now, we’re running Kyro from opposite sides of the world: Latin America and Asia.

Our Mission

We’re on a mission to make fitness accessible globally. Whether you’re in Tokyo, Buenos Aires, or Cape Town, Kyro helps you keep your routine on track and discover new fitness communities.

What Kyro Does

Kyro connects travelers, remote workers, and fitness lovers to gyms around the world. With just a few taps, find and book a gym wherever you are — no contracts, no hassle.
